Enneagram Type: The Perceptive Specialist

Core Motivation - To understand and to make sense of the world

Type-Based Strengths
  • Calming presence – are able to remain calm in a crisis as they detach from their emotions
  • Complex Problem Solver- think through complex problems in great detail to come up with practical solutions
  • Perceptive & Insightful - are very observational and aware of their environment
  • Learning Orientation - the desire to constantly learn and pick up new skills and abilities can make them good at taking up new and complex projects


Under stress they may show the following behaviours
  • Withdrawn & Aloof – may retreat from interaction & may spend longer time alone
  • Detached/difficulty relating to emotions - may focus more on the action, information and data rather than the emotional impact of the situation/decision
  • Slow to move into action if unsure – in situations where they do not have the expertise, and there may be ambiguity, they may face difficulty
  • Condescending – can display a know-it-all attitude and become challenging to influence unless relevant data is produced
