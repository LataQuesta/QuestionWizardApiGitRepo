Enneagram Type: Receptive Peacemaker

Core Motivation - To be settled and in harmony with the world

Type-Based Strengths
  • Calmness and adaptability - have a calming presence and are able to adapt to many different situations
  • Supportiveness - are very good at supporting and reassuring those around them
  • Mediating - can mediate between others to resolve conflict to restore peace
  • Open-mindedness - Due to their desire to keep the peace, they are open-minded, suspending judgement and seeing things from multiple perspectives


Under stress they may show the following behaviours
  • Procrastinator – can display indecisiveness when unable to get consensus from others and hence delay action/reaction to a situation
  • Passive-aggressive – when feeling pressured or when they find it difficult to put their viewpoint across may resort to passive-aggressiveness
  • Unassertive – tend to put their needs on the back burner and focus on what others want; can be self-deprecating and undervaluing their own contributions
  • Ambivalent – can respond in ways that are non-committal and can confuse others as to whether they agree or disagree with them
