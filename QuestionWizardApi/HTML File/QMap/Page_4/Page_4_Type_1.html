Enneagram Type : The Ethical Perfectionist

Core Motivation - To be good/to do the right thing

Type-Based Strengths
  • Rational and reasonable - tend to proceed only on logic and are objective, which makes them a great addition to any work environment
  • Principled - want to do what is right, stick to the truth, value justice and want to be fair and ethical
  • Purposeful - tend to spend a lot of time thinking about their actions and the impact of those actions on the outcome and/or on others
  • Are improvement-oriented – paying attention to details and errors so that they can help and connect with the wider community


Under stress they may show the following behaviours
  • Tend to see things in black and white, and this can cause difficulty in working with ambiguity
  • Rigid and inflexible – thinking that their way is the right way
  • Resistant to change - can be condescending and remote
  • Unempathetic and demanding – their dogmatic and critical approach can be seen as lacking in empathy
  • Overly Critical – constantly focusing on correcting self and others
