Q1. What do you do when you go to parties? Can you walk up to people and start a conversation? Can you tell us about a time you did this and how it went for you?
Q. Rigidity - Is it easy for you to work with someone who has different ideas than you? Do you feel uneasy/irritated when someone doesn’t work according to your standards? Q. Close-mindedness is it easy for you to come up with multiple solutions or do you think there is only one correct way of doing something?
