Enneagram Type : The Ambitious Achiever

Core Motivation - To outshine and be the best hence focus goes to tasks and projects and how to finish them in the most efficient ways to look good in the eyes of others.

  • Extremely ambitious and competitive
  • Want to excel at everything and be seen as successful
  • Are adaptable and self-assured, focused on setting & achieving goals
  • Appearances matter a lot to them
  • Attention, recognition & admiration keeps them motivated
  • Quick to read a room & grasp the power relationships
  • Winning and being efficient is very important
  • Can at times cut corners

What they value in a role/org culture?

What can cause disengagement?

  • They seek an achievement-oriented, fast-paced, and energy-driven organisation
  • Outlining of a clear mission, vision, and purpose with a clear goal and reward system
  • Where the successful are admired, appreciated and recognised for their contributions
  • Ambiguous goals or targets; unclear standards of delivery/expectations
  • Slow pace; lack of competitive targets
  • Too much dependence on others to get the work done
  • A caring or too empathetic culture that values people over achievement

Decision Making Style

Interpersonal Style

  • Place emphasis on research but can get irritated if the team is caught in 'analysis paralysis'
  • Focus on finding the most efficient and practical solution
  • Their decisions are usually clear, efficient, and well-conceived
  • Assertive, outgoing; socially confident & self-assured
  • Identify what others see as success & calibrate their presentation to match that image
  • Impatient with lengthy conversations
  • When they speak, people listen to them, their energy is contagious & motivating

Conflict Management Style

Leadership Style

  • Self-reliant and based on competency - data, facts, and information
  • Expect everyone to be task & goal oriented; focus on finding the most efficient and practical solution
  • Decisions that will lead to success and present them as outstanding
  • May go behind people's backs to garner support from influential people
  • Bring the confidence to reach the goal and achieve success
  • Move things along and speed up the pace when slowdown occurs
  • Know how to read the audience and can help clarify what will sell and what won't
  • Cannot stop working or slow down which may lead to mistakes, overwork, burnout, and stress
