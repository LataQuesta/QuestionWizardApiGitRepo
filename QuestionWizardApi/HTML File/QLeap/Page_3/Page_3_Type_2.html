Enneagram Type : The Empathic Nurturer

Core Motivation - To be liked and appreciated hence focus goes to people around them and how to best forge positive relations by managing people and interactions.

  • Are extremely warm and giving
  • Want to help and be of service to others; gravitating towards those in distress
  • Have an innate ability to make people feel safe and comfortable
  • Work on developing mutually nurturing and sustainable relationships
  • Bring enthusiasm and passion to their work
  • Can be great mentors and networkers
  • Brings in the strength of emotional intelligence
  • Can get along with people well

What they value in a role/org culture?

What can cause disengagement?

  • Where collaboration/ people contact and open communication is given importance
  • Group work/teamwork takes precedence over individual contribution
  • Their kindness is noticed & appreciated; can bring about a positive change by helping everyone
  • Where due credit and appreciation for contribution is not given
  • Excessive focus is on profits and targets at the cost of people and relationships
  • Working in silos or independently with low collaboration
  • High competition and lack of empathy

Decision Making Style

Interpersonal Style

  • Focus is on the impact of the decision on the feelings of others keeping in mind their best interest
  • Focus is on the emotional dynamics at play
  • Find it difficult to take a call when they see that their decision can generate resistance or negatively impact people
  • Can easily enlist others to follow their decisions
  • They focus on building relationships
  • Ready to step in and help others even when they are inundated with work
  • They compliment and appreciate others more frequently which acts as a positive reinforcement of others' behaviours
  • Acknowledge others' feelings and needs
  • Give kind & encouraging feedback but often neglect difficult yet important conversations

Conflict Management Style

Leadership Style

  • They strive to resolve conflicts through cooperation
  • If highly stressed, can become aggressive and volatile
  • May choose to walk-out when confronted with big emotions
  • Will take time out to bring all the facts and issues to discuss before moving towards resolution, sometimes leading to delays
  • Are very inspiring as they bring passion and enthusiasm to their work, especially if they find meaning in it
  • Make people feel valued & sincerely want people to enjoy working with each other
  • Value work-life balance and will place a high priority on making the workplace more compassionate
  • Find it extremely hard to deliver negative feedback and often tolerate bad behaviour
