Enneagram Type: The Versatile Visionary

Core Motivation - To gain experiences and avoid pain hence focus goes to finding an unexpected exciting connection between things and charming people with their fast-paced flexible thinking.

  • Have a mind buzzing with ideas thoughts, plans, and actions
  • Can quickly synthesize information and multi-task
  • Can be impatient and over-extend themselves
  • Can be visionaries, who can energize the team and can be good contributors towards achieving the vision of organization
  • Can be charismatic, innovative, and creative
  • Don't like too many hierarchies and do not want to deal with unpleasant emotions at work
  • Prefer to look at the bigger picture, leaving the tedious task of execution at times to others

What they value in a role/org culture?

What can demotivate them?

  • Cultures that offer lots of freedom and flexibility to do work their way
  • Environments that are dynamic, ever-changing, where they are able to think creatively; brainstorm about the future
  • Role/Culture that allows them to wear multiple hats, offers fast-paced projects with shorter deadlines
  • Prefer cultures with more focus on people than hierarchies
  • Rigid work environments or overly bureaucratic ones where they are micro-managed and given lots of routine, repetitive tasks
  • Where supervisors/bosses are autocratic and domineering and not open to the ideas they may have
  • Slow pace in general, with little connection to people can easily bring down their motivation levels

Decision Making Style

Interpersonal Style

  • Like to examine possibilities & entertain their options; hence are adept at creating multiple pathways towards the solution
  • Come up with creative ideas during crisis situations
  • When stressed, they can be overly anxious or impulsive about decisions
  • Resent being rushed and may snap at people who push them to take a call
  • Have a story-telling style that captures people's attention and influences others
  • They think, talk & do things fast, making it difficult for others to keep up
  • Having a positive outlook makes them upbeat, exciting & a stimulating company
  • They like to relate to people, engage in enjoyable activities and help others visualise the future in an imaginative way

Conflict Management Style

Leadership Style

  • To avoid feeling unpleasant they may totally avoid conflicts or may seek shortcuts
  • May find it hard to focus on the other person's story
  • Usually, may retreat first, then become demanding
  • May make exaggerated commitments which may end up in poor follow-through
  • Their positivity lifts the morale & encourages others to envision a successful result
  • Do not dwell on what's not working, focus on solutions and are open to listening to others
  • Are inspirational and visionary leaders getting people to do things they felt were impossible
  • May get so excited about ideas that they don't slow down to examine potential problems and develop a workable implementation plan
