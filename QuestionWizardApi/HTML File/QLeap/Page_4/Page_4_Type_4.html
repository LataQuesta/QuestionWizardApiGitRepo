Enneagram Type: The Intense Individualist

Core Motivation - To be unique and authentic hence focus goes to their connectedness to others, to evaluating the authenticity of things and translating emotional depth into creative acts of self-expression.

Type-based Strengths
  • Sensitivity - are sensitive to and understand the feelings of others
  • Introspective and Self-awareness - demonstrate the ability to connect deeply with their own emotions and have an awareness of their growth areas
  • Creative and Innovative - are imaginative and deep-thinkers. They bring depth, creativity, and individuality to the workplace
  • Idealistic & Inspiring - can build a vision and influence and inspire others to work together to achieve the goal

Typical challenges for people who work with them
  • Fours can harbour grudges if they feel wronged by someone
  • They may appear self-centred and self-absorbed
  • They can get upset when they feel they are not understood or supported by others
  • Fours can come across as negative and pessimistic and people feel like they have to walk on egg-shells around them

Under stress they may show the following behaviours
  • Deeply Critical of self and others - under stress, they may tend to become deeply critical of their own flaws and zoom into the flaws of others
  • Moody & Temperamental - can withdraw from the world - interaction may be limited or minimal, can be aloof or distant and demonstrate mood swings
  • Pessimism â€“ can become overly pessimistic, have a negative outlook or approach to things, everything gets shaded in the negative lens
  • Unrealistic and Impractical - in their desire to do something unique and innovative, they may push themselves to the extent where they come up with really unrealistic and impractical solutions and ideas

What could negatively impact their performance
  • Are idealistic but can also be impractical, unrealistic, and far-fetched in their innovative ideas
  • Their hunger for excellence while being a strength can become a derailer when they become moody and temperamental expecting everyone to be on the same page as them
  • Are known for their compassionate nature but they can get overwhelmed with others feelings and emotions
  • Their need for authenticity may sometimes come across as idiosyncratic behaviour to others
