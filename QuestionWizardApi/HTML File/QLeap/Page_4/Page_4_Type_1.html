Enneagram Type : The Ethical Perfectionist

Core Motivation - To be good/to do the right thing hence focus goes to the gap between how something is and to what it could be and what would be required to bridge the gap.

Type-based Strengths
  • Rational and reasonable - tend to proceed only on logic and are objective, which makes them a great addition to any work environment
  • Principled - want to do what is right, stick to the truth, value justice and want to be fair and ethical
  • Purposeful - tend to spend a lot of time thinking about their actions and the impact of those actions on the outcome and/or on others
  • Are improvement-oriented - paying attention to details and errors so that they can help and connect with the wider community

Typical challenges for people who work with them
  • When they get annoyed, Ones can be self-righteous and unbending
  • When upset, Ones can withdraw and give others the silent treatment
  • Ones can take the moral high ground believing that their way is the only right way
  • Ones can get tense and stressed when discussing a point of disagreement

Under stress they may show the following behaviours
  • Tend to see things in black and white and this can cause difficulty in working with ambiguity
  • Rigid and inflexible - thinking that their way is the right way
  • Resistant to change - can be condescending and remote
  • Unempathetic and demanding - their dogmatic and critical approach can be seen as lacking in empathy
  • Overly Critical - focusing constantly on correcting self and others

What could negatively impact their performance
  • Their conscientiousness can turn into rigidity and inflexibility. They can become strict with themselves and others, leading to extended hours and burnout for self and even others
  • A preference for a structured and methodical approach to execution can make it difficult for them to perform in a constantly changing environment
  • Can become highly judgmental of others and critical of self and others
  • Quality focus can lead to exceeding deadlines
