Enneagram Type: The Charismatic Controller

Core Motivation - To be strong and in control/charge hence focus goes to the big-picture, to what they think should happen and how to make that vision a reality.

Type-based Strengths
  • Confidence - have a lot of confidence in themselves, their skills and abilities
  • Strength - demonstrate courage and steel - can stand up for themselves and others around them
  • Decisiveness - have strong decision-making skills which tend to make them great leaders
  • Have the ability to take control of both their own lives and their situations
  • Demonstrate grit and perseverance, taking on impossible challenges

Typical challenges for people who work with them
  • Eights can sometimes be so intense that they might come across as intimidating
  • They may talk a lot, but not listen
  • Eights may be so focused on the big picture that they miss important details
  • They may not be able to empathise with colleagues who are having a difficult emotional experience

Under stress they may show the following behaviours
  • Confrontational - can become argumentative and rigid, not wanting to budge from their stance; may engage in verbal/physical confrontation
  • Controlling - can become excessively demanding as well as controlling; wanting to be kept posted about everything, may not give enough freedom to others
  • Impatient and angry - can become impatient with a slow pace at work and/or with the slow pace of others as well; can come across as irritable and angry
  • May not show any kind of vulnerability which leads others’ to distance themselves from them

What could negatively impact their performance
  • Can be straightforward, but can also become arrogant, lacking empathy, not knowing the impact of their words and actions on others
  • Decisiveness when taken to extremes can make others feel that their opinions are disregarded and are of no value
  • High action-orientation can lead to impulsive behaviour and decision-making if not supported by reflective thinking
  • Their influencing nature can lead them to become domineering, controlling, and stubborn
