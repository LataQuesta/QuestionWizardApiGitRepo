Enneagram Type : The Ambitious Achiever

Core Motivation - To outshine and be the best hence focus goes to tasks and projects and how to finish them in the most efficient ways to look good in the eyes of others.

Type-based Strengths
  • Adaptability – they thrive working with people in different settings
  • Goal-Setting – they need to be successful, hence they are good at setting goals and working towards them
  • Practical – they work efficiently towards workplace goals; are pragmatic and practical
  • Charming personality – they are highly energised, confident, and engaging at the workplace

Typical challenges for people who work with them
  • Threes can get burnout from overwork, becoming impatient with the needs and emotions of others
  • Can become frustrated and dismissive in meetings if they think that their time is being wasted
  • Can make mistakes if they don't slow down causing others to rework and complete the tasks
  • Threes can become so competitive that the drive to win clouds their judgement

Under stress they may show the following behaviours
  • Overly Competitive - can make the environment cut-throat or even cut corners
  • Lower on Empathy - are more task-oriented than people-oriented; tend to keep emotions at bay
  • Superficial & Utilitarian - can stay at the surface-level and not invest too much time or energy in tasks/relationships
  • Over-exert & cause Burnout - in their desire to achieve all goals they can push themselves and others to burnout

What could negatively impact their performance
  • Their competitiveness can make them cut corners and be impersonal in their business dealing with others
  • Their industriousness can make them a workaholic and cause burnout to them and the people working with them
  • Being pragmatic, they may sometimes be low on innovation and miss an opportunity because of lack of creative thinking
  • Their goal-oriented behaviour can sometimes make them impatient and superficial with others
