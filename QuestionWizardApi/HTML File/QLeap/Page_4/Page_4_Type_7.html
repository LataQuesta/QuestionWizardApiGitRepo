Enneagram Type: The Versatile Visionary

Core Motivation - To gain experiences and avoid pain hence focus goes to finding an unexpected exciting connection between things and charming people with their fast-paced flexible thinking.

Type-based Strengths
  • Goal-orientation - are motivated by new experiences and brainstorm ideas when working towards a goal
  • Sociable personalities - have high levels of energy; enjoy meeting and interacting with new people
  • Problem-solving - have the ability to see many options and lots of possible solutions to a problem
  • Adaptability - have the ability to learn new skills and can easily handle changes in plans; are quick and creative thinkers.

Typical challenges for people who work with them
  • Sevens may not have the patience or emotional fortitude to identify problems, talk them through and work things out
  • They may have difficulty focusing on projects that don’t excite them and can get easily bored
  • May not provide much structure or direction for their direct reports as their desire for freedom is more important to Sevens
  • They like to keep their options open and so may not commit to a plan of action in a timely way

Under stress they may show the following behaviours
  • Scattered and Unfocused - Can become indecisive, spread themselves thin and all over the place with multiple ongoing projects
  • Avoid Painful Situations - find it difficult to give constructive feedback or get into any unpleasant conversations if they feel it will cause a bad mood
  • Low on Empathy - are focused on quickly moving on from unpleasant emotions and hence may be unwilling to hear out the challenges of others
  • Rationalize Negative Experiences - to the point that they may refuse to see what is obvious to others

What could negatively impact their performance
  • Have a capacity for quick thinking which when over-used can make them distracted and unfocused
  • Their need for innovation may at times make it difficult for them to complete tasks causing them to quickly move from one idea to another
  • Are very resilient, but when taken to extremes, they can become escapists or can persist with unattainable goals
  • They bring the skill of networking, which when over-used can make them overly assertive, arrogant, and pushovers
