Enneagram Type: The Perceptive Specialist

Core Motivation - To understand things by gathering knowledge and hence focus goes to data and facts, and how to produce results and solve complex problems by collecting and analysing the best information.

Type-based Strengths
  • Calming presence - are able to remain calm in a crisis situation as they detach from their emotions
  • Complex Problem Solver - think through complex problems in great detail to come up with practical solutions
  • Perceptive & Insightful - are very observational and aware of their environment
  • Learning Orientation - the desire to constantly learnÂ and pick up new skills and abilities can make them good at taking up new and complex projects

Typical challenges for people who work with them
  • Fives can appear insensitive and aloof to the emotions of others
  • They can undervalue others opinions, especially if they aren't presented in a dispassionate way
  • They may not always want to collaborate, they guard against feeling dependent on someone they don't trust to do the job right
  • Fives need for compartmentalisation may seem excessive and controlling to others who don't delineate to the extent to which Type fives do

Under stress they may show the following behaviours
  • Withdrawn & Aloof - may retreat from interaction & may spend longer time alone
  • Detached/difficulty relating to emotions - may focus more on action, information and data rather the emotional impact of the situation/decision
  • Slow to move into action if unsure - in situations where they do not have expertise and there may be ambiguity, they may face difficulty
  • Condescending - can display a know-it-all attitude and become difficult to influence unless relevant data is produced

What could negatively impact their performance
  • Are highly perceptive, but their thoughtfulness can also be taken for being withdrawn and private
  • Their need for objectivity makes them draw logical conclusions but can also lead to indecisiveness and lack of creative thinking
  • Are good at giving autonomy to their team members but may not give sufficient direction & inputs
  • Their independent mindedness though a strength can be taken to an extreme where they can be low on empathy and emotionally distant
