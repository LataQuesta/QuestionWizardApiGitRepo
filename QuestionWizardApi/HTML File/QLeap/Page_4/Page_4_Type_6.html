Enneagram Type: The Dutiful Loyalist

Core Motivation - To be safe and secure hence focus goes to reading people and situations, to determine how trustworthy and safe they are.

Type-based Strengths
  • Responsibility - tend to be sensible and make practical choices
  • Commitment - honor their commitments to people and plans
  • Team spirit - seek cooperation, guidance and support, consider both logic and emotion
  • Caring - deeply care about others and will protect them offering a safe, secure, and nurturing environment

Typical challenges for people who work with them
  • Sixes slow down the work process when they ask too many questions; finding it difficult to move forward if their questions are not answered
  • They tend to make things more complicated than they need to be; their constant worrying can become taxing for people dealing with them
  • They can get stuck in ambivalence and hesitation and have a difficult time moving into action getting stuck in analysis-paralysis
  • Their cynical attitude can often feel like criticism and resistance

Under stress they may show the following behaviours
  • Worrying & Anxious - become suspicious of others' intentions and agendas; focus more on the worst-case scenario and what can go wrong
  • Reactive or Defensive - become hyper-vigilant and feel threatened, insecure and may behave impulsively
  • Analysis-paralysis - may not trust self or others and overthink things
  • Defiant & Rebellious - may keep their guards up, go against the authority, raise questions

What could negatively impact their performance
  • Are cautious & careful but, can become indecisive, unassertive, hyper-vigilant, and delay their actions
  • Their responsibility and commitment to others when taken to an extreme can cause emotional exhaustion and difficulty in letting go when needed
  • Are dutiful and loyal, and sometimes may sacrifice personal goals for the sake of others
  • Their collaborative nature can become a challenge making it difficult for them to work alone and making them dependent on others
